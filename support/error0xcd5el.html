Error 0xCD5EL

Fluorine 1

Short description: Fluorine 1 encountered an unexpected issue originating from the control panel. This error indicates the control panel failed or produced an invalid response during a control sequence required by the app.

This error is very common!

What this means

  • The control panel sent an unexpected or malformed response while Fluorine 1 attempted to perform a control operation.
  • Try and restart the Control Panel.
  • If this issue persists, restart the application and you will be good to go
